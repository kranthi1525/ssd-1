Take-Off Process

At Squad Steel Detailing, our take-off process is rooted in the precise quantification of steel members, connections, and materials directly from design documents. Using state-of-the-art BIM software, our detailers create comprehensive 3D models that faithfully represent the final structure. This advanced approach ensures the accuracy of all material quantities, significantly reducing the chances of shortages or excesses during fabrication and erection. By streamlining this process, we help optimize resource allocation, minimize waste, and ensure smooth project execution from start to finish.
